Changes in Thinknowlogy 2012 (release 2) compared to Thinknowlogy 2012 (release 1):
- Bug fixes;
- Added detection of Semantical Ambiguity (static as well as dynamic);
- Slightly improved question answering (of "is" answering);
- Slightly improved processing of personal pronouns;
- Improved notifications in the Connect-Four game;
- Improved reasoning:
	- Drawing Conclusions on possessive sentences;
	- and making more Assumptions on generalizations.
	Both result in improved justification reports.
